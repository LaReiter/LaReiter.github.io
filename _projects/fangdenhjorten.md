The game was developed during the summer, 2013. My small nephew was chasing my brother Morten, who had taken his soccer ball. Kids sometimes misspronounce names, and Morten easily came out sounding like "Hjorten". The title of the game thus became "Fang Den Hjorten" (Catch that Hjorten).
