Hi, I'm Lars Nørtoft Reiter

Statistician · Physicist · PhD
{%- assign about_page = site.pages | where: "name", "about.md" | first -%}

I'm endlessly curious about how the world works — from the synchronized movements of fish schools and the invisible dark matter filling our universe to the unspoken rules of human behaviour. That curiosity took me through physics and into statistics, where I now build models to make sense of messy, real-world data.

Alongside the research, I write essays and reader's letters on science, reasoning, and the quiet ways numbers shape our decisions. Below you'll find my education, publications, and selected writing — and you can read more about me here.
